SOCIAL ANIMALS

Social animals are those in which individuals of the same species live together cooeratively in an organised community.

A colony is a group of individuals belonging in the same species and organised in a coperative manner

EXAMPLES ARE:

  • Termites
  • Bees
  • Humans
  • Ants
  • Foxes

CHARACTERISICS OF SOCIAL INSECTS.

  1. They belong to the same species
  2. they live together in a coperative manner in a colony
  3. They display division of labour
  4. They display dinstinct castles
